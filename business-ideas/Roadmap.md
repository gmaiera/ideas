# Roadmap

This roadmap tracks how business ideas move from raw concept to validation, pilot, and operating company.

## Now

- Shape the Fork/Piggyback thesis into a partner-ready concept.
- Choose one beachhead vertical.
- Choose one narrow workflow for the first demo.
- Create a trust and data governance checklist.
- Draft the first partner pitch.

## Next

- Interview 5 operators or partners in the chosen vertical.
- Build a lightweight demo of the AI intake and work-packet flow.
- Model three commercial structures: revenue share, margin share, and platform fee plus success fee.
- Identify 10 possible first partners.
- Decide whether Fork or Piggyback is the primary name for external conversations.

## Later

- Run one partner pilot.
- Convert pilot learnings into a repeatable vertical playbook.
- Build the first operating dashboard.
- Define the reusable platform layer.
- Decide whether the company is primarily a joint-venture studio, services firm, or SaaS platform.

## Parking Lot

- Public landing page.
- Investor memo.
- Brand identity.
- Multi-vertical expansion.

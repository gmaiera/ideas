# Business Ideas

This is the working area for raw, active, paused, and archived business ideas.

## Workflow

1. Capture rough ideas in `Inbox.md`.
2. Promote promising ideas into their own file.
3. Track active bets in `Active.md`.
4. Use `Roadmap.md` for staged priorities.
5. Use `Backlog.md` for actionable tasks.
6. Put time-based follow-ups in `../reminders/Reminders.md`.

## Statuses

- `raw`: captured but not yet shaped
- `researching`: worth investigating
- `validated`: has meaningful evidence
- `active`: currently being built, sold, or tested
- `paused`: intentionally parked
- `discarded`: rejected with a reason

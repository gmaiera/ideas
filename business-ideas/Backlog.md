# Backlog

This backlog contains actionable tasks for business ideas. Keep reminders in `../reminders/Reminders.md`; keep tasks here.

## Fork / Piggyback

### Research

- Pick the first vertical to test: accounting, legal-adjacent services, insurance brokerage, HR/payroll, compliance, or managed services.
- List 20 repeatable workflows in the chosen vertical.
- Score workflows by revenue potential, repeatability, regulatory complexity, data sensitivity, and ease of demo.
- Research how AI implementation firms, BPOs, and vertical SaaS companies price similar partnerships.
- Identify 10 potential first partners.

### Strategy

- Decide whether the first wedge is new revenue, margin improvement, faster delivery, or AI transformation with low operational risk.
- Define the minimum access needed to build a useful operating twin.
- Create the first version of the partner economics model.
- Define what Fork/Piggyback promises not to do with partner data.
- Decide whether the company should be partner-branded, co-branded, or invisible behind the incumbent.

### Product

- Sketch the AI intake flow.
- Sketch the work-packet creation flow.
- Sketch the human review and approval flow.
- Sketch the operating dashboard.
- Define the first demo dataset using synthetic or anonymized data.

### Sales

- Draft a one-page partner pitch.
- Draft a 5-slide partner conversation deck.
- Write a cold outreach message for partners.
- Write a warmer intro version for people already in the network.
- Prepare objections and responses around data access, competition risk, quality, and economics.

### Legal And Trust

- Draft a data governance checklist.
- Define consent requirements for emails, transcripts, documents, and customer data.
- Define audit logging requirements.
- Define human review requirements for regulated or high-risk work.
- Identify legal review topics before any real pilot.

### Brand

- Compare Fork and Piggyback as external names.
- Decide whether "We want to fork you" is internal-only, campaign-specific, or public.
- Draft safer tagline options.
- Draft a short explanation of the name Piggyback.
